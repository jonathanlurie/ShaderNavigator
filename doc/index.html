Build a TextureChunk object.

constuctor(index3D: THREE.Vector3, resolutionLevel: number, sizeWC: [number](default 1), sizeVoxel: [number](default 64))
Parameters
index3D (THREE.Vector3) The index position in the octree. Each members are interger. This is a THREE.Vector3
resolutionLevel (number) The level of resolution, the lower the level, the lower the resolution. Level n has a metric resolution per voxel twice lower/poorer than level n+1, as a result, level n has 8 time less chunks than level n+1 (remember we are in 3D!).
sizeWC ([number](default 1)) Size of the chunk in world coordinates.
sizeVoxel ([number](default 64)) Number of voxel per side of the chunk.
